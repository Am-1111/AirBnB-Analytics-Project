## Requirements
- Software: Tableau Desktop (Download from [Tableau](https://www.tableau.com/)).
- Data Source: [Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/alexanderfreberg/airbnb-listings-2016-dataset)
- System Requirements:
  - Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 (or newer)
  - Memory: At least 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)
  - Disk Space: Minimum 5 GB of free space
- Dependencies:
  - No additional dependencies required for Tableau Workbook.
